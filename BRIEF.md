## 1. What is BEAT (in one paragraph) — UPDATED 27 Apr

BEAT is LP&ET's program for competition baristas and brewers. **For Edition III (2026): 20 boxes × 6 kg = 120 kg total of green coffee**, awarded to 20 selected competitors. It becomes a **public contest**: competitors submit a video pitch, public votes via likes, jury scores on a 30/30/20/20 rubric. Winners receive a BEAT box for their next competition + LP&ET co-amplifies their story across channels. Tied to feria Japón Oct 2026 + WC Brewers Cup Colombia 2027.

> **NOTE:** The legacy "FTH Beat" subscription program (100 boxes/year at $750) is separate. The 2026 contest gives away **20 boxes** as a curated relaunch.

## 4. Key principles (from CraftLab + research)

| # | Principle | Manifestation in BEAT |
|---|-----------|----------------------|
| 1 | Evidence-first over marketing claims | Show actual SCA scores of past BEAT lots, real names of past competitors |
| 2 | Qualitative tiers, not raw numbers | Submission tiers `Strong / Promising / Needs work` (not "7.4/10") |
| 3 | Methodology disclosure (Strava) | Dedicated rubric explainer with weights + judges named |
| 4 | Self-skepticism (Parker) | "What we look for / What we don't" honest section |
| 5 | Live preview (sticky) | Submission preview card updates as form fills |
| 6 | Glance / Deep dive toggle | Each section expandable for mobile vs deep |
| 7 | SDT — Autonomy / Competence / Relatedness | Choose your story, kit guides you, connection to LP&ET team |
| 8 | NO XP/levels/coins | "Score" + "Rank" only — competition-native language |
| 9 | Selective sharing | Form lets competitor pick public vs jury-only fields |
| 10 | Microinteractions explicit | "Video link verified" / "Submission locked" feedback |
| 11 | Progressive disclosure (3 layers) | Hero → Mid → Deep |
| 12 | Performance budget | LCP <2.5s, all images via Cloudinary `f_auto,q_auto` |

### Section 6 — Your video must include
Bullet list pulled directly from Jeffry's spec:
- Country + name + biggest career achievement
- Preparation of your favorite drink (any method)
- Pitch: how your vibe aligns with BEAT + LP&ET
- Closing CTA: "vote for me, follow @lapalmayeltucan, #[TBD]"
- **Background:** none — clean text section, max readability

### Section 7 — How we judge (rubric)
| Criterion | Weight | What we look for |
|-----------|--------|------------------|
| Technical mastery | 30% | Precision, cleanliness, workflow |
| Storytelling + the why | 30% | Personal connection to LP&ET values |
| Communication quality | 20% | Camera presence, technical clarity |
| Impact potential | 20% | Creativity, editing, vibe |
| Bonus | extra | Top 5 most-liked videos |

Plus disclaimer (Parker pattern): "What we don't look for: hyped editing, flashy gear, perfect English. We look for sincerity and craft."

### Section 8 — Submission form
- Multi-step form with sticky preview card
- Fields: name, country, IG handle, video link (YouTube/Vimeo/Drive), category (barista / brewer / aeropress / other), elevator pitch text (200 chars), email, consent
- Privacy: email + phone marked "jury only"
- Real-time validation: video link parsed, IG handle verified
- **Microcopy:** "Your submission will be locked. You'll hear back within 14 days."
